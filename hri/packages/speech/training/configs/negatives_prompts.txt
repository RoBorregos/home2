# Prompts to read aloud when recording the "negatives" set.
# The point is to expose the model to words and phrases that sound close to
# yes / no / stop / frida but must NOT trigger. Read each line naturally,
# varying pitch and speed. One line ≈ one clip (2 seconds is plenty).
#
# Hard-negative candidates (phonetically close):
yesterday
unless
i know
you know
no problem
no way
nope
stopped
stopping
don't stop
free the
frida kahlo
freedom
friday
afraid
front of me
# Normal conversational sentences (so the classifier learns "speech that is
# not a command" looks like this):
the robot is moving to the kitchen
please take the cup from the table
the weather is nice today
i am looking for my phone
can you hand me the book
thank you very much for your help
she went to the store this morning
let me think about it for a second
i would like some coffee please
put the box in the corner of the room
# Long sentences containing the target words in non-command context:
yesterday i was thinking about the project
we know that the answer is correct
there is no reason to worry
i think we should stop here and take a break
